Seo terminologies-

1.Backlink-
   backlink is simply a link from one website to another.Search engines looks if  there is a link on a website of  another website means that website trusts  content of refring website. So backlink  helps in increasing authrority of your website.

2.Bot

bot si the crawler which access the pages of website .

3.Canonical url- 

 canonical url means a preffered website url, when there are multitple url with similar content.

most important guidelines to do seo of any website

1.Give page name according to property or work or keyword for          example about.html ,gallery.html
2.Title name should be used three or four times in page .
3.Keep navigation and description information in header tag.
4.Keep footer  credit name as link.
5.Comment your website link on others already ranked site.
6.Add attribute  in img tag (most important to rank images)

8.upload sitemap to google search console.
9.Bold keyword in description.
10.Set keyword in
  1. first heading
  2. first line of first paragraph
11.Key word in conclusion of your post or page.
12.Minimum keyword density should be 1% and maximum 2%,
     example- for 1000 words 20 keyword.
13.If you want to priorities specific keyword then place that in              initial lines of your post or page.
